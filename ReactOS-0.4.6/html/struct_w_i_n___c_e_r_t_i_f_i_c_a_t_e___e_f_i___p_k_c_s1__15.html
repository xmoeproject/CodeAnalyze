详细描述

Certificate which encapsulates the RSASSA_PKCS1-v1_5 digital signature.

The WIN_CERTIFICATE_UEFI_PKCS1_15 structure is derived from WIN_CERTIFICATE and encapsulate the information needed to implement the RSASSA-PKCS1-v1_5 digital signature algorithm as specified in RFC2437.
