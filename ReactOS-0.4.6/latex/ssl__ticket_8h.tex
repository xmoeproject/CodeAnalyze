\begin{DoxyNote}{注解}
\hyperlink{class_it}{It} is highly recommended to select a cipher that is at least as strong as the the strongest ciphersuite supported. Usually that means a 256-\/bit key.

The lifetime of the keys is twice the lifetime of tickets. \hyperlink{class_it}{It} is recommended to pick a reasonnable lifetime so as not to negate the benefits of forward secrecy.
\end{DoxyNote}
